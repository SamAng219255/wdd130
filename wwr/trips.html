Trips

Have questions or ready to book your next adventure? Contact our team today and let us help you plan an unforgettable white water rafting experience!

Thunderwhirl Canyon Express

Gear up for a heart-racing half-day sprint through the roaring rapids of Thunderwhirl Canyon. This high-adrenaline excursion packs Class III–IV whitewater into a 3-hour voyage, complete with breathtaking cliffside views and a splash-filled finale at “Devil's Drop.” Perfect for thrill-seekers who want maximum excitement on a tight schedule.

Family Float Adventure

Bring the whole crew for a gentle, scenic float down Willow Bend. Over two relaxing hours, you'll drift past serene sandbars, spot native wildlife, and even stop for a riverside picnic. Ideal for families, beginners, and anyone who prefers to soak in the river's beauty at an easy, leisurely pace.

Ridge to Rapids Expedition

Embark on our flagship full-day journey that starts with a sunrise hike to “Eagle's View” overlook and concludes with an epic descent through thunderous Class IV–V rapids. Along the way, enjoy guide-led geology talks, lunch on a hidden riverbank, and an unforgettable sunset paddle back to basecamp.
