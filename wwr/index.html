At Thunder Ridge White Water Rafting, we believe everyone deserves a taste of adventure. Our mission is to make the wild beauty of the river accessible and unforgettable—no matter your skill level. Since 1998, we've combined safety, excitement, and deep respect for nature into every guided trip we lead.

From gentle family floats to heart-pounding Class V rapids, we offer guided rafting experiences for every adventurer. Explore the scenic Thunderwhirl Canyon, tackle roaring whitewater, or enjoy a laid-back paddle—all with our expert guides leading the way.
